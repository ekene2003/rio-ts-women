what we do

our services

At The Memunat Single Mother and Child Initiative, we believe that single mothers and their children deserve respect, support, and opportunities to thrive. Our slogan is “…empathise, not stigmatise.” We provide a range of services to help single mothers and their children rebuild their lives socially and economically through education or vocational/entrepreneurship skills acquisition and digital education.

we offer services in different feilds

education

We believe that education is the key to breaking cycles of poverty and disadvantage. Our initiative provides access to school supplies, scholarships, and mentorship programs for children of single mothers, helping them achieve academic success and brighter futures.

Digital Education

In today’s world, digital skills are essential. We run training sessions and workshops that introduce single mothers and children to computer literacy, online learning, and basic tech skills. This equips families to compete in the digital age and access more opportunities.

Psycho-Social & Mental Health Support

We understand the emotional and psychological challenges faced by single mothers and their children. Our psycho-social and mental health services offer professional counseling, safe spaces for peer support, and empowerment programs that help families build resilience, improve well-being, and overcome social stigma.

Nutritional Support

Proper nutrition is vital for the health and development of both mothers and children. We provide nutritional assistance through food drives, meal support programs, and awareness campaigns on healthy living, ensuring that no child goes hungry and every mother has the strength to provide.

Why Our Services Matter

  • We bridge gaps in education and digital literacy.
  • We provide holistic support for mental, emotional, and social well-being.
  • We ensure both mothers and children have access to proper nutrition.
  • We work to empower families and secure brighter futures.
